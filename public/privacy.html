Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Raheel Ahmad ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit raheelab.com and the AI Product Academy pages, or enroll in our programs.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you provide: your name and email address when you opt in, contact us, or enroll.
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, actions taken, device and browser information, and approximate location, collected automatically via cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver the course, process enrollment, and send transactional and onboarding emails.
  • To measure, operate, and improve our website and marketing.
  • To show and optimize relevant advertising on platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google.

3. Cookies & tracking technologies

We use cookies and pixels — including the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics — to understand how visitors use the site and to measure and improve advertising. We also use server-side conversion measurement (Meta Conversions API). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-party services

We share limited data with trusted providers solely to operate our service:

  • Meta Platforms — advertising and conversion measurement (privacy policy).
  • Google — analytics (privacy policy).
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy).
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.

Data shared for advertising measurement (such as a hashed email or event identifiers) is used to match and report conversions and is handled per each provider's terms.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To opt out of marketing emails, use the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us directly.

6. Data retention & security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, and we use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it.

7. Children

Our services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 18.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
